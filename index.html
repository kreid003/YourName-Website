HLG Event Director

Hello, my name is Kevin Reid and I am the Event Director here at UCR. My job here is to create and plan events which are hosted by our gaming club here on campus for students, while also teach them what esports is and how to get more involved in them if they so choose. Events are very important for student because it gets them involved in activites that they might have not have before. These events also teach students concepts about esports and other gaming related topics. During the first quarter of being in this position, I have helped create a couple of events. The first one was at the first week of school and was located at a local resturant off campus. This social was a Super Smash Bros. themed event. It was a social, but with more smash related activites there. Our Esports Director helped students learn about what it takes to compete at a high level for this game. He explained many techniques and strategies which helped students learn how to further grow their own skill than what they had before. During the event we also had tryouts for the official UCR Smash team. Our team captain as well as other Club officiers helped choose the select few to represent UCR. HLG's biggest event was also this quarter. This event was called Highlander Con and brought many companies,such as Nvidia and HyperX on campus to talk about their products and what they do in the field of Esports. The event also hosted many great giveaways and brought togther many gamers and non gamers alike. Altogether, representing HLG as Event Director is something I am proud of and is something I want to continuesly help grow and improve.
